**Anmaya Technologies Private Limited** is an Indian technology and education company based in Udupi, Karnataka. The company focuses on advanced hardware engineering, semiconductor design, telecommunications, FPGA development, RISC-V systems, and industry-oriented technical education.

Through its training division, **VIVEQA**, Anmaya provides hands-on learning programs in FPGA Design, VLSI, Embedded Systems, AI Hardware Acceleration, 5G Communication, and High-Performance Computing (HPC).
